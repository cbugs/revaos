Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 ozs. butter
  • 3 ozs. sugar
  • 1/2 lb. honey
  • 1 teaspooonful baking powder

Preparation

  1. Cream the butter and sugar together; beat in the eggs, one at a time.
  2. Add the honey, and mix thoroughly.
  3. Sieve the flour and baking-powder together, stir them into the cake mixture.
  4. When well mixed, turn into a cake-tin that has been lined with paper.
  5. Press it down well, make the top level, and put into a moderate oven; bake for about one hour.
  6. When done turn out of the tin, remove the paper, and then place on a sieve to cool.
